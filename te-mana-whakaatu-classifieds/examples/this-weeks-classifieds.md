# This Week's Classifieds

*Ratings supplied under the Te Mana Whakaatu classification framework.
Illustrative parody — invented ads, not affiliated with or produced by the
real Classification Office of New Zealand. Demonstrates the rating/
descriptor format and the "reasons for decision" aside described in
`../SKILL.md`.*

---

**GARAGE SALE — Sat 8am, 14 Kōwhai Street.** Moving overseas, everything
must go. Recliner (good condition, minor cat damage), lawnmower
(temperamental), full set of encyclopedias (1994, mostly complete), and a
heated dispute with the neighbour over whose fence it actually is.

**RATING: PG**
*Contains coarse language (one instance, directed at a lawnmower that
would not start), mild interpersonal conflict of a boundary-dispute
nature, and unresolved questions regarding the recliner's true age.*

---

**LOST: GINGER CAT, "BISCUIT."** Last seen Tuesday near the reserve,
answers to his name if he feels like it, does not feel like it most of the
time. Missed dearly. Reward offered, mostly emotional.

**RATING: G**
*Contains mild emotional intensity and a reward structure the advertiser
has not fully thought through.*

---

**FLATMATE WANTED.** Sunny room, close to bus, one existing flatmate
(drummer, mostly considerate). No smokers. Must be okay with a moderately
opinionated cockatiel.

**RATING: M**
*Contains references to percussion at inconsistent hours, a cockatiel
with strong and frequently expressed views, and a level of social
negotiation some viewers may find intense.*

**Reasons for decision:** This ad was initially rated PG on submission.
Upon further review, the phrase "mostly considerate" was judged to imply
enough unresolved domestic tension to warrant elevation to M. The
classification stands.

---

**CAR FOR SALE.** 2009 hatchback, WOF till November, small dent (long
story), reliable once it warms up. First reasonable offer takes it, and by
reasonable we mean reasonable.

**RATING: PG**
*Contains an unexplained dent, mild suspense regarding cold-start
reliability, and a pricing negotiation that may test the patience of all
parties.*

---

**LOST UMBRELLA**, large, black, sentimental value (it was a gift, don't
ask). Left at the library or possibly the bus, honestly it's a blur. No
reward, just the satisfaction of doing a good deed.

**RATING: G**
*Contains mild nostalgia and a narrator who admits, unprompted, to not
remembering the material facts of the case.*

---

*This section is a work of comedic parody. Te Mana Whakaatu is the real
Classification Office of New Zealand; this publication is not produced,
endorsed, or reviewed by them, and none of the above ads, ratings, or
"reasons for decision" are genuine classification decisions.*
